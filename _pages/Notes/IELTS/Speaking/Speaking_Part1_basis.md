# SpeakingPart1必考题
为了方便大家理解，我会把work&study直接拆分成work、study。

Where you live目前有三种提问范围，可以分成hometown、area where you live、place where you live。

## 一、work
### 1、What work do you do?你做什么工作?/Are you working or are you a student？
> I'm an HR specialist at a tech company. I mainly recruit new employees and mediate workplace conflicts. Sometimes, I also help with onboarding to make sure new hires settle in smoothly. The job can be tricky because I have to juggle different personalities, but I enjoy being the go-to person for career advice. It’s great to see employees thrive and contribute to the company.
我是 一家科技公司的 Hr.我的主要工作是 招聘新员工并调解职场矛盾。有时，我也会负责新员工入职，确保他们能 顺利适应。这份工作有时不太容易，因为我要同时应对不同性格的人，但我喜欢成为大家信赖的职业顾问。看到员工成长并为公司做贡献，让我觉得很有成就感。

> I'm a software engineer at a startup. I spend most of my time coding and troubleshooting bugs. My team and I build AI-powered apps that aim to make life easier. The job can be intense when we’re on a crunch, but I love the problem-solving aspect. Nothing beats the thrill of debugging all night and finally cracking the issue.
我是 一家初创公司的程序员。我大部分时间都在 写代码和排查 bug.我的团队开发 人工智能应用，目标是 让生活更便捷。有时工作压力会很大，尤其是 赶项目 的时候，但我特别喜欢 解决问题的过程。没有什么比 熬夜调试后成功找到 bug 更爽的了！

### 2、Do you like your job?你喜欢你的工作吗? / Have you always wanted to do this kind of work?
> Yes, I do! I love working in HR because I get to interact with people every day. It’s rewarding to help employees find the right career path and create a positive work environment. Sometimes, dealing with difficult employees can be a headache, but I’ve learned to handle tricky situations with patience. At the end of the day, seeing people grow and succeed makes all the challenges worth it.
是的，我很喜欢！我喜欢 人力资源工作，因为每天都能 和不同的人打交道。帮助员工找到合适的职业方向，并 营造积极的工作环境，让我很有成就感。当然，有时候 处理棘手员工 确实会让我头疼，但我学会了 用耐心解决复杂问题。最终，看到别人 成长并取得成功，让我觉得所有的挑战都是值得的。

> Yes, for the most part! I enjoy coding and solving complex problems—it feels like cracking a puzzle. And it’s also fun to work on projects that can actually make a difference in people’s lives. That being said, debugging for hours can be exhausting, especially when nothing seems to work. But when I finally get the code to run smoothly, the sense of achievement is unbeatable.
是的，大部分时间都喜欢！我喜欢 写代码和解决复杂问题，这感觉就像 破解谜题。而且，能参与 有实际影响力的项目，让我觉得很有趣。当然，调试代码几个小时 会很累，特别是当 怎么改都不对 的时候。但当代码 最终运行流畅，那种成就感 真的无可比拟。

> My day usually starts with a quick team meeting to discuss our tasks. After that, I spend most of my time coding and debugging. If I run into a problem, I brainstorm solutions with my teammates. In the afternoon, I might have another meeting to review our progress. Sometimes, I get so deep into coding that I forget to take breaks!
我的一天通常从快速的团队会议开始，我们会讨论当天的任务。之后，我大部分时间都在写代码和调试。如果遇到问题,我会和队友一起头脑风暴找解决方案。下午，我们可能会再开个会，回顾项目进展。有时候，我写代码写得太专注，甚至忘了休息！

### 6、**Do you work best in the morning or the afternoon?** 
> I work best in the morning. My mind is fresh, and I can focus better on important tasks like screening resumes or planning interviews. I usually grab a coffee and power through my to-do list before lunchtime. In the afternoon, I start feeling a bit drained, so I save that time for meetings or less demanding tasks. A good start in the morning makes the whole day more productive.
我早上的工作效率最高。我的思维更清晰，更容易专注于重要任务，比如筛选简历或安排面试。我通常喝杯咖啡，然后 一鼓作气完成待办事项。到了下午，我开始有点疲惫，所以更适合开会或处理轻松的任务。早上如果状态好，一整天都会更高效。

> I’m more productive in the afternoon. It takes me a while to fully wake up in the morning, so I usually start the day with small tasks like checking emails. After lunch, I get into the zone and can write code for hours without distraction. Some of my best ideas come late in the day when I’m fully warmed up. That’s why I prefer tackling complex problems in the afternoon.
我下午的工作效率更高。早上我需要一点时间才能完全清醒，所以通常先处理邮件这样的小任务。午饭后，我进入状态，可以连续写好几个小时的代码，而且不受干扰。我的最佳灵感往往出现在下午状态完全调整好之后。所以，我更喜欢下午解决复杂问题。
